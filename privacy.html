Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your professional data

1. Data Collection

We collect personal and professional information necessary for job applications and immigration support. This includes contact details, professional experience, educational background, and identification documents required for Canadian employment verification.

2. Information Usage

Your data is used exclusively for the purpose of matching you with professional opportunities in Canada and facilitating the application process with potential employers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All sensitive documents are stored in encrypted environments.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze platform performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our data protection officer at privacy@canadaopportunityhub.com.
