Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about finding career opportunities in North America.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, you first need to create a free account on our platform. Once registered, you can browse available listings and submit your application along with your CV and supporting documents.

Do I need a work permit before applying?

While some employers prefer candidates who already have work authorization, many of the opportunities on our hub include employer sponsorship for qualified candidates. We provide expert guidance on the permit application process.

What documents are required?

Generally, you will need a valid passport, an updated CV in North American format, educational transcripts, and proof of work experience. Specific requirements may vary by job category.

Is there a fee for using Global Hub?

Account creation and job browsing are completely free for all candidates. We offer premium services for resume optimization and personalized immigration consulting which may involve service fees.

How long does the process take?

The timeline varies significantly depending on the job type, your country of origin, and the specific immigration pathway. On average, the process can take anywhere from 4 to 12 months.
